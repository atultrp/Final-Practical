About College

    Acharya Narendra Dev College Quick Updates

  • Beyond the chalk and talk teaching practices, the college always takes a step forward with their new pedagogical techniques. That includes project based learning, theatre, film making, debating, audio recording, discussion, photography etc.

  • The college provides classrooms together with IT and Multimedia facilities. The students are provided laptops for their benefits and also to encourage the self-learning system since the Operating System is 'Open Source'. In order to facilitate the Open Source Software in education the college provides training to the new entrants.
